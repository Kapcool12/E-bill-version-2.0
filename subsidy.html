Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a resident of the service area.
  • Annual household income must be below $50,000.
  • Must not have any outstanding electricity bills.
  • Must be a senior citizen (65 years or older) or a person with disabilities.
  • Proof of income and residency must be provided.
