Frequently Asked Questions

eBill is an online service that allows you to view, pay, and manage your electricity bills conveniently.

To sign up for eBill, go to the signup page, fill out the required personal and connection information, and submit the form.

You can pay your bill online by logging into your account, navigating to the 'Pay Bill' section, and following the payment instructions.

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, and online banking.

You can contact our customer support team by visiting the 'Contact Us' page and filling out the contact form, or by calling our support hotline.
